## Overview

A regulatory-finance simulation engine built in collaboration with California Energy and Power (CE+P). The platform models how carbon intensity scores and credit market dynamics affect renewable fuel project economics under California's LCFS program and the federal Renewable Fuel Standard (RFS). ## Assumptions Framework

The model is structured around three tiers of assumptions to maintain credibility and avoid speculative claims:

**Tier 1 â€” Structurally Safe (Regulatory Reality)**
Public policy mechanics modeled as structural truths:
- LCFS credits awarded based on carbon intensity differential
- LCFS credits priced per metric ton of COâ‚‚e avoided
- RINs tied to renewable fuel production under the federal RFS
- Ethanol project revenue streams: fuel sales, LCFS credits, RINs
- Standard project finance metrics: EBITDA, IRR, DSCR, NPV

**Tier 2 â€” Parameterized Inputs (Configurable)**
All variables are user-editable inputs, not fixed assumptions:
- CI score, CI reduction from carbon capture
- Ethanol price, feedstock cost, LCFS price, RIN price
- Plant capacity, capex, debt ratio, interest rate, operating costs

**Tier 3 â€” Avoided**
No proprietary claims, insider assumptions, or unverified company-specific data. All scenarios framed as hypothetical California ethanol projects.

## Core System Architecture

### 1. Modeling Engine (Backend)
- LCFS and RIN credit revenue calculation
- Annual revenue aggregation with opex/capex modeling
- Debt amortization and capital structure modeling
- IRR, NPV, and DSCR computation
- Sensitivity analysis and optional Monte Carlo simulation

### 2. Data Layer
- Public LCFS and RIN historical price data (sourced and labeled)
- Ethanol and corn futures reference pricing
- Manual CSV upload as fallback for custom datasets
- Clearly labeled as historical reference, not predictive

### 3. Frontend

**Section A â€” Executive Overview**
Revenue breakdown, IRR, DSCR, carbon intensity, and credit revenue contribution. Clean and minimal.

**Section B â€” Sensitivity Explorer**
Interactive sliders for CI score, LCFS price, feedstock cost, ethanol price, and capex. Visual outputs include IRR sensitivity curves, tornado charts, and revenue stack bars.

**Section C â€” Risk Simulator (Advanced)**
Monte Carlo simulation with configurable volatility distributions, 1,000+ simulation runs, IRR distribution visualization, and probability analysis against hurdle rates.
